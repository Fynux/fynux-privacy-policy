Tunee – Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains in detail how Tunee, developed and operated by Fynux ("we", "us", "our"), handles and protects any information accessed during your use of the app.

1. Information We Access

Tunee is an offline music player. It accesses audio files and metadata stored locally on your device but does not collect, transmit, or store any data externally.

2. Permissions

  • Storage/Media Access: Required to list and play your music.
  • Notification Access (Optional): To show playback controls.

3. No Account Required

Tunee does not require registration or login. No account or identity is linked to app usage.

4. Ads & Analytics

No ads. No analytics. 100% offline and private.

5. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is shared or uploaded.

7. Changes

We may revise this policy and update it here at any time.
